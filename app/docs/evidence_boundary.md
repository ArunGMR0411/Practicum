# App Evidence Boundary

The CASTLE Privacy Pipeline App is a bounded research demonstrator.

## Supported Claim

The app demonstrates that the project pipeline can process real image batches, route frames through deterministic privacy methods, and produce auditable logs.

OAPR is the adaptive scientific framework evaluated in the thesis. The App operationalises evidence-selected visual-safe methods through simplified `objective_profile` presets suitable for demonstration and research handover. App runs support demonstrator validation only and do not redefine scientific results.

## Not Claimed

- It is not a production DCU deployment.
- It does not guarantee full anonymisation.
- It does not prove the Objective-Aware Privacy Router globally beats fixed blur.
- It does not run every advanced generative branch live in the demonstrator.
- It does not claim true race, gender, or age preservation.

## Correct Wording

Use:

- "privacy-risk reduction"
- "graceful degradation across supported compute profiles"
- "resource-derived concurrency rather than a fixed cap"
- "apparent demographic consistency"
- "bounded app demonstrator"
- "objective profile" for App Privacy/Balanced/Utility presets
- "scientific OAPR" only when referring to the locked thesis framework and evidence

Avoid:

- "production-ready"
- "full anonymisation"
- "works on any system"
- "globally beats blur"
- "race/gender/age preservation"

## Advanced-Method Boundary

- Diffusion is full comparable evidence but remains visual-quality gated.
- Reverse Personalization is partial comparable evidence because 18/500 frames
  still failed after targeted retry and runtime remained high.
- NullFace has full metric evidence but remains quality/runtime-limited and non-default.

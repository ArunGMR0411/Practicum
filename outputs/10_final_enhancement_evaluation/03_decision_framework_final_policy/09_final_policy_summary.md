# Final policy-selection layer (progressive evaluation)

The **gated decision framework** is the formal final policy-selection layer.
**Exploratory composites** remain comparison evidence, not deployable defaults.

## Components

- Atomic metrics
- Hard safety gates (failure, visual eligibility, privacy floors, recall floor)
- Multimodal miss→0 privacy for missed modalities
- Sensitivity analysis
- Pareto analysis
- Final deployment eligibility ranking
